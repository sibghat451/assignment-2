(Why to Boycott Israel Products)

Boycotting Israeli products represents a symbolic and tangible protest against the Israeli government's treatment of Palestinians. It's a stance against the occupation of Palestinian territories, illegal settlements, and human rights violations, including restrictions on movement, land confiscation, and discriminatory laws.
By refusing to purchase Israeli goods, individuals aim to exert economic pressure to compel Israel to reconsider its policies and engage in constructive dialogue towards a just and peaceful resolution to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.

Social justice cannot be attained by violence. Violence kills what it intends to create. [...] The way to peace is a simple choice: boycott Israel until it complies with international law.
